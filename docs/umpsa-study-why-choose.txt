Vice-Chancellor's Welcome:
Welcome to Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah (UMPSA). As a focused university specializing in engineering, technology, and industrial sciences, UMPSA offers cutting-edge programmes in high demand within Malaysia's fast-growing economy. We prioritize a student-centred, problem-based approach to teaching, complemented by state-of-the-art facilities and a 'Teaching Factory' concept that simulates real-world industry environments. With two campuses in Pekan and Gambang, UMPSA provides a dynamic setting for both learning and research, fostering a conducive environment for scholars.
