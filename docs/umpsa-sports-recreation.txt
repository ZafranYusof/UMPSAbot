OVERVIEW
========
Pusat Sukan dan Kebudayaan UMPSA (Sports and Culture Centre) manages sports facilities and activities across both Gambang and Pekan campuses.

FACILITIES (Both Campuses)
===========================
Sports facilities are available at both Gambang and Pekan campuses. The centre manages:
- Indoor sports halls
- Outdoor sports fields
- Swimming facilities
- Gymnasium
- Courts (badminton, tennis, futsal, basketball, volleyball)
- Athletic track
- Football/rugby fields

EVENT MANAGEMENT
================
The Sports Centre also handles event management services including:
- Karnival Sukan (Sports Carnival)
- Varsity leagues (e.g., Varsity Sepak Takraw League)
- SUKMA preparation (Sukan Malaysia / National Games)
- Cross-country championships (Kejohanan Merentas Desa)
- Inter-university sports (MASUM)
