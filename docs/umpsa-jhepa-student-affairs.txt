OVERVIEW
========
Jabatan Hal Ehwal Pelajar & Alumni (JHEPA) was established in 2002. JHEPA is responsible for managing student welfare and development in support of the university's vision, mission, philosophy and core values.

Motto: "Kebijaksanaan Memacu Kejayaan" / "Wisdom Drives Excellence"

VISION
======
Ke arah memperkasa pelajar yang berpengetahuan, berketrampilan, beradab yang menyumbang kepada kesejahteraan sejagat.
(Towards empowering students who are knowledgeable, skilled, and well-mannered, contributing to global well-being)

COMMITMENTS
===========
1. Menyediakan perkhidmatan yang mesra dan responsif (Providing friendly and responsive services)
2. Menggalakkan penglibatan pelajar dalam program pembangunan sahsiah dan kepimpinan (Encouraging student participation in character and leadership development programs)
3. Menjalin dan mengukuhkan hubungan dua hala antara universiti dan alumni (Building and strengthening two-way relationships between university and alumni)
4. Mewujudkan persekitaran kediaman pelajar yang sejahtera (Creating a conducive student residential environment)
5. Menguruskan keperluan kebajikan pelajar secara profesional (Managing student welfare needs professionally)
6. Menyediakan peluang dan ruang peningkatan bakat kepada pelajar untuk melibatkan diri dalam aktiviti kebudayaan secara aktif (Providing opportunities for students to actively participate in cultural activities)
