Source: https://jhepa.umpsa.edu.my/ , https://edasar.umpsa.edu.my/jhepa
Last updated: 2026

OVERVIEW
========
Student discipline at UMPSA is governed by JHEPA (Jabatan Hal Ehwal Pelajar & Alumni) through the Akademi Adab (Etiquette Academy) and related departments.

GOVERNING BODY
==============
- Akademi Adab (Etiquette Academy) - Director: Dr. Siti Umairah binti Mokhtar
- Part of JHEPA under the Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Student Affairs & Alumni)

STUDENT POLICIES & REGULATIONS
================================
Student policies are documented in the eDasar UMPSA system:
- Website: https://edasar.umpsa.edu.my/jhepa
- Contains official university policies related to student affairs

Key areas covered:
1. Student conduct and behaviour expectations
2. Academic integrity
3. Residential rules and regulations
4. Dress code guidelines
5. Campus safety rules
6. Disciplinary procedures and penalties
7. Student rights and responsibilities
8. Anti-harassment policies
9. Substance abuse policies
10. Use of university facilities

DISCIPLINARY PROCESS
=====================
- Violations reported to JHEPA
- Investigation conducted
- Disciplinary committee hearing
- Penalties may include warning, suspension, or expulsion depending on severity
- Appeals process available
