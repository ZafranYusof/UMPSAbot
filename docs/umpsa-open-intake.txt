UMPSA Open Intake Programme

Website: https://open-intake.umpsa.edu.my

The Open Intake Programme is a specialized platform for full-time UMPSA degree, Foundation, and Diploma programmes, providing an alternative pathway for both local and international students to gain admission to a public university without the need for the standard UPU system.

Managed by: UMPSA Advanced

Who Can Apply:

For Degree Programme:
- Local applicants with: Diploma, Matriculation, STPM, Foundation, or equivalents
- International candidates with: High School Certificates (HSC), A-Levels, or equivalent qualifications

For Foundation and Diploma Courses:
- Local SPM leavers and equivalents
- International students with O-Level qualifications or equivalents

Key Features:
- Alternative pathway to public university admission
- No need for standard UPU system
- Full-time programmes
- High-quality, accredited programmes
- Applications submitted online through My Credential

This initiative is designed to attract more qualified students to UMPSA's high-quality, accredited programmes, offering them a valuable opportunity to secure admission to a public university.
