1. TYPES OF FINANCIAL AID AVAILABLE
================================================================================

A) PINJAMAN PTPTN (National Education Loan)
   - Covers tuition fees and living costs
   - For Malaysian citizens, age ≤45, household income ≤RM50,000/month
   - UMPSA students (Universiti Awam):
     * Diploma: up to RM4,750/year
     * Degree (Sains): up to RM6,650/year
     * Degree (Sastera): up to RM6,180/year
     * Master: up to RM9,500/year
     * PhD: up to RM24,700/year
   - Loan tiers: Maximum (STR recipients), 75% (income ≤RM8k), 50% (income >RM8k)
   - NEW 2026: Loans converted to full scholarships for e-Kasih families at IPTA
   - Apply via PTPTN online portal (need SSPN Prime 15-digit account)

B) BIASISWA YAYASAN UMPSA (UMPSA Foundation Scholarship)
   - For excellent students at Bachelor's Degree level
   - Application opens annually (usually March-April)
   - Includes English Written Test as part of selection
   - Open to all UMPSA degree students

C) BANTUAN AWAL INSENTIF PENDIDIKAN (Early Education Incentive)
   - RM1,000 from Yayasan UMPSA & Yayasan Perkasa Siswa
   - For new students from B40 families

D) BANTUAN KOMPUTER RIBA (Laptop Assistance)
   - Free laptop for students who cannot afford one
   - Applied through JHEPA

E) BANTUAN CARE4U YAYASAN PERKASA SISWA
   - RM200 for students in need

F) BANTUAN YAYASAN PELAJARAN MARA & TAJAAN EKUITI NASIONAL
   - RM2,500 for eligible students

G) BANTUAN DERMASISWA B40 TVET KPT
   - Full tuition fee coverage
   - Monthly allowance throughout studies
   - For B40 students in TVET programmes

H) INSENTIF KECEMERLANGAN YAYASAN UMPSA
   - For 150 excellent students from diploma/matrikulasi/politeknik/STPM
   - Awarded each academic session

I) BANTUAN ZAKAT PELAJAR (Muslim students)
   - Financial aid from zakat funds
   - Application opens every semester via JHEPA
   - For Muslim students in financial need

J) BANTUAN SARA DIRI (Non-Muslim students)
   - Equivalent welfare aid for non-Muslim students
   - Application opens every semester via JHEPA

K) BANTUAN MUSIBAH (Emergency/Disaster Aid)
   - For students facing emergencies or disasters

L) eKUPON KASEH RAHMAH (Free Food Coupons)
   - Free meals at selected cafeterias (Gambang & Pekan campuses)
   - Via Adab@UMPSA app
   - For B40 students facing financial difficulty

M) UMPSA CAMPUS PANTRY & DAPUR SISWA
   - Food assistance facilities on campus

N) SKIM PERANTISAN SISWA (Job on Campus)
   - Part-time work opportunities within campus

O) PROGRAM PEMBANGUNAN SISWA SULUNG (SULUNG)
   - Special support for first-in-family university students from B40

P) PINJAMAN PELAJARAN NEGERI SELANGOR
   - State education loan for students from Selangor

Q) EXTERNAL SCHOLARSHIPS (advertised via Study@UMPSA):
   - Program Ijazah Dalam Negara (PIDN)
   - Biasiswa Yang di-Pertuan Agong (BYDPA)
   - Biasiswa Tunku Abdul Rahman
   - Maybank Group Scholarship Programme

2. HOW TO APPLY
================================================================================

PTPTN:
- Portal: https://www.ptptn.gov.my
- Need SSPN Prime account (15 digits)
- Apply online after receiving university offer letter
- Guide: https://sspnportal.com/langkah-permohonan-pinjaman/

UMPSA INTERNAL AID (Zakat, Sara Diri, Bantuan Kewangan):
- Check announcements at: https://edasar.umpsa.edu.my (JHEPA section)
- Apply through Adab@UMPSA app
- Follow HEPA UMPSA Malaysia on Facebook for updates
- Applications typically open each semester

BIASISWA YAYASAN UMPSA:
- Watch for announcements on Yayasan UMPSA Facebook page
- Application usually opens March-April each year
- Includes written test and interview

EXTERNAL SCHOLARSHIPS:
- Check: https://study.umpsa.edu.my/index.php/financial-aid
- Each has its own application portal and deadline

3. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA (GENERAL)
================================================================================

PTPTN:
- Malaysian citizen
- Age ≤ 45
- Household income ≤ RM50,000/month (gross, minus RM250/dependent)
- Enrolled in approved programme
- GPA ≥ 2.0 (for continued disbursement)
- Must have SSPN Prime account

UMPSA Internal Aid:
- Registered UMPSA student
- B40 family background (priority)
- Financial need demonstrated
- Muslim (for zakat) / Non-Muslim (for sara diri)
- Good academic standing

Yayasan UMPSA Scholarship:
- Excellent academic results
- Bachelor's Degree student at UMPSA
- English proficiency (tested)
