HOSTEL APPLICATION GUIDELINES - UMPSA

URL: https://gading.ump.edu.my/ump/f?p=116

How to access: Go to Student Life > Student Residence > Hostel Application.

=== APPLICATION FOR NEW STUDENT ===

Step 1: Browse to https://gading.ump.edu.my/ump/f?p=116 or go to Student Life and search for Student Residence then Hostel Application.

Step 2: View your Student Details at the main page for Hostel Application and click on APPLY NEW STUDENT.

Step 3: Select the HOSTEL, BLOCK, LEVEL, ROOM AND BED according to your campus and availability capacity. The student residences listed are already sorted by gender. Refer to the legend to check for availability.

Step 4: After selecting the residence, a popup of validation will appear. Read carefully and understand all the conditions stated. If you agree with the conditions and all information is correct, click on the checkbox.

Step 5: Click on SUBMIT to proceed with the application. Once submitted, you will receive a HOSTEL SLIP. Print this slip on the day of hostel key collection.

Step 6: After submitting, you can check your details again by clicking on the MAIN button and identify roommates in the Room/Hostel Details section.
