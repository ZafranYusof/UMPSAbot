CYCLE@CAMPUS PROGRAMME
======================
UMPSA encourages students to walk and cycle on campus as part of its green campus initiative.

Campaigns:
- Regular campaigns to encourage walking and cycling
- Flyers distributed to pedestrians and cyclists for awareness about UMP Green Campus
- "Jom Kongsi Jalan Raya" (Let's Share the Road) campaign
  - Awareness campaign for car and motorcycle owners to reduce speed while driving inside campuses

GREEN TRANSPORTATION INITIATIVES
=================================
Part of UMPSA's UI GreenMetric sustainability efforts:
- Promoting zero/low emission transportation
- Encouraging cycling and walking
- Speed reduction campaigns within campus
