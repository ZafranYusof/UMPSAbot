UMPSA Vice-Chancellor's Welcome Message
=========================================

Vice-Chancellor: Professor Ts. Dr. Yatimah Alias

Welcome to Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah (UMPSA)

ABOUT UMPSA
------------
UMPSA is a vibrant, forward-thinking institution committed to academic excellence, impactful research, and meaningful industry collaboration. It aspires to be a leading provider of engineering and technology-based education, research, and innovation.

LONG-TERM VISION (Three Transformative Aspirations):
1. Eco-University by 2030 - championing sustainability and environmental stewardship
2. ASEAN Advanced TVET Hub by 2030 - driving excellence in technical and vocational education and training across the region
3. Distinguished Technical University by 2050 - known globally for world-class programmes and innovation-driven impact

SPIROS PHILOSOPHY
-----------------
A holistic and balanced approach that empowers every member of the UMPSA community:
- Skills
- Perfection
- Impact
- Resilience
- Optimisation
- Sustainability

Guiding slogan: "Technology for Society"

UNIVERSITY FOCUS
----------------
- Specialisation in engineering, technology, and industrial sciences
- Student-centred, problem-based learning approach
- Industry-ready, globally competitive, and ethically grounded graduates
- Faculty of Industrial Management complements technical strengths with business and management acumen
- Advanced facilities bridging academic theory and industrial practice
- 'Teaching Factory' concept simulating real-world industry environments
