UMPSA Dual Degree Programmes

Website: https://dualdegree.umpsa.edu.my

Benefits of Dual Degree Programme:
- Awarded a Dual Degree (UMPSA & Collaboration University)
- Career Opportunities in Multinational Companies Around the World
- Practical Oriented Education
- Academic Staff Who Have Industrial Experience
- Recognized by Malaysian Accreditation Body and International
- Exposure to Abroad Teaching, Learning and Industrial Training

Partner Universities:
- Karlsruhe University of Applied Sciences (HKA), Germany
- Mannheim University of Applied Sciences (HMA), Germany - ESB Business School
- BJTU, China
- Tianjin University, China
- Kazakh National Agrarian Research University (KazNARU), Kazakhstan
- Hanyang University Erica Campus, South Korea

Available Dual Degree Programmes include:
- Bachelor of Business Engineering With Honours (with HMA Germany)
- Automotive Engineering German Dual Degree

Programme Structure (example - Automotive Engineering):
- 9 semesters total
- Includes 6 months internship at companies like Continental Automotive Component
- 1 semester conducting internationally funded team-oriented project study
- 1 semester studying in Karlsruhe, Germany

Student Testimonials:
Students from diverse backgrounds including Orang Asal communities have successfully completed the programme and secured positions at multinational companies like TF-AMD in Penang.
