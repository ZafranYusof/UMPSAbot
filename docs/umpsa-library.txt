Services:

1. DISCOVERY@LIB:
"Your one-stop platform to search, discover, and access everything in one place!"

2. eLib System (Digital Library):
Collections include:
- eBook
- Thesis
- Open Shelf
- CD & DVD
- Reference
- PTC (Past Year Collection)
- Special Collection
- FYP (Final Year Project)

3. INTER LIBRARY LOAN & eDDS SERVICES:
ILL and eDDS services are offered to all registered users. The library assists users in retrieving materials either in its original form or copies from the library collection regardless if it is available or not.

4. UMPSA-IR (Institutional Repository):
For depositing articles/items to UMPSA-IR. For post-graduates of UMPSA, all submission of articles or conference papers may be emailed to:
- amelia@umpsa.edu.my
- norsaini@umpsa.edu.my

5. eLIB eBOOK Portal:
Users can borrow and read books digitally with 3 easy steps:
1. Register account
2. Borrow book
3. Read book
