INTERNATIONAL DUAL DEGREE Notes:
- Students will be directly offered the dual degree programme (for some programmes)
- For other programmes: initially offered as single degree, then in the second semester, students who meet the requirements will be offered the dual degree programme
- A new offer letter will be issued if the student agrees to accept the dual degree offer

UMPSA TVET BORNEO (OFFSHORE):
- Total semesters: 7 semesters
- 3 semesters at UMS Sabah
- 2 semesters at UMPSA Pahang
- 2 semesters WORK-BASED LEARNING (WBL) / Industrial Training

ASASI - UMPSA ADVANCED:
- Programme: Foundation in Science and Technology
- Under the supervision of UMPSA Advanced Education (University's subsidiary)
- Applications are made directly through the Open Channel (not via UPUONLINE system)
- This programme is offered without government subsidy
- Apply at: https://open-intake.umpsa.edu.my/
