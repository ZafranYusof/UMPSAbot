================================================================================
PROGRAM DITAWARKAN
================================================================================

PROGRAM SARJANA MUDA (UNDERGRADUATE):

1. Bachelor of Computer Science (Software Engineering) with Honours
   - Kod: BCS (SE)
   - Tempoh: 4 tahun (8 semester)
   - Akreditasi: MQA, BEM
   - Fokus: Software development, system design, project management

2. Bachelor of Computer Science (Computer Systems & Networking) with Honours
   - Kod: BCS (CSN)
   - Tempoh: 4 tahun (8 semester)
   - Akreditasi: MQA
   - Fokus: Network security, cloud computing, system administration

3. Bachelor of Computer Science (Graphic & Multimedia Technology) with Honours
   - Kod: BCS (GMT)
   - Tempoh: 4 tahun (8 semester)
   - Akreditasi: MQA
   - Fokus: UI/UX design, game development, multimedia applications

4. Diploma in Computer Science
   - Tempoh: 3 tahun (6 semester)
   - Fokus: Asas pengaturcaraan, pangkalan data, rangkaian

PROGRAM PASCA SISWAZAH (POSTGRADUATE):

1. Master of Computer Science (by Coursework)
   - Tempoh: 1.5 - 2 tahun
   
2. Master of Science (Computer Science) - by Research
   - Tempoh: 2 - 4 tahun

3. Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Computer Science
   - Tempoh: 3 - 6 tahun

KEMASUKAN & SYARAT
================================================================================

Syarat Am Sarjana Muda:
- SPM: Lulus BM, Sejarah, Matematik, dan Matematik Tambahan
- Minimum 5 kredit termasuk Matematik dan satu subjek Sains
- STPM/Matrikulasi/Diploma yang berkaitan
- MUET Band 2 minimum

Syarat Khas:
- Matematik Tambahan (kredit/lulus bergantung program)
- Fizik atau subjek berkaitan ICT
