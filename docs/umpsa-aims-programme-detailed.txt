UMPSA AIMS Programme (ASEAN International Mobility for Students)
=================================================================

Website: https://www.umpsa.edu.my/en/international/aims-asean-international-mobility-students-programme

OVERVIEW
--------
The ASEAN International Mobility for Students (AIMS) Programme is a regional student mobility initiative coordinated by SEAMEO RIHED, aimed at strengthening academic cooperation and people-to-people connectivity among higher education institutions in ASEAN and selected partner countries.

HISTORY
-------
- Originated in 2009 as the Malaysia-Indonesia-Thailand (M-I-T) Student Mobility Pilot Project
- Has since expanded significantly
- As of 2025, involves multiple ASEAN member states and partner countries
- Supports short-term, credit-bearing student mobility across a wide range of academic disciplines

PARTICIPATING COUNTRIES (as of 2025)
-------------------------------------
- Malaysia
- Indonesia
- Thailand
- Vietnam
- Philippines
- Brunei Darussalam
- Japan
- Republic of Korea

HOST UNIVERSITY SUPPORT
------------------------
Under the AIMS Programme, the Host University generally provides:
- Tuition fee waiver
- Access to basic medical services (campus or in-house health centres)
- Assistance in arranging student accommodation
- Student support services (buddy, tutoring, and counselling programmes)

FINANCIAL SUPPORT
-----------------
Financial support is typically provided by the Home Government / Home University, subject to national policies and availability of funds. This may include:
- Monthly living allowance
- International airfare (economy class, subject to approval)
- Visa and insurance support
- Other allowable mobility-related expenses

Note: The type and amount of financial assistance may vary by country and institution.
