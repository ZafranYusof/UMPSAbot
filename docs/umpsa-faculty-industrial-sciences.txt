Dean's Welcome:
FIST is committed in its effort to ensure quality and standard of all its Undergraduate and Postgraduate Academic Programs to fulfil the country's manpower needs as the nation advances towards 2025 to become a high-income economy. FIST strives to ensure its academic programs and research are tailored towards producing holistic, entrepreneurial, and balanced graduates in line with the Malaysian Education Blueprint 2015-2025 and UMP Strategic Plan 2021-2025.

Dean: Associate Prof. Dr. Aizi Nor Mazila Binti Ramli
