AUDITORIUM
==========

Pekan Campus:
- Located at 2nd floor of the Library
- Capacity: 200 persons
- Equipment:
  - Visualizer
  - LCD screen and projector
  - Sound Reinforce System (SRS)
  - Stage lighting system
  - AV equipment

Gambang Campus:
- Located at 1st floor of the Library
- Capacity: 200 persons
- Equipment:
  - Visualizer
  - LCD screen and projector
  - Sound Reinforce System (SRS)
  - Stage lighting system
  - AV equipment
- Reservation: Contact Media Unit

TEACHING FACTORY
================
Students in UMPSA enjoy the use of state-of-the-art facilities that resemble those used in industries. Known for its "Teaching Factory" concept, students in UMPSA are taught by experienced faculties in a learning environment that prepares them for the real-world.

MULTIPURPOSE HALL
=================
Available at both campuses for events, activities, and gatherings.

MOSQUE
======
Available at both Pekan and Gambang campuses for Muslim students and staff.
