UMPSA Outbound Mobility Programmes
====================================

Website: https://www.umpsa.edu.my/en/international/outbound-mobility-programmes

OVERVIEW
--------
UMPSA Outbound Mobility Programmes provide students with valuable international learning experiences that help them gain knowledge, skills, and cultural exposure to thrive in a global environment.

Selection of students is merit-based, considering both academic performance and personal qualities such as independence, maturity, initiative, and the ability to represent UMPSA and Malaysia positively.

Participants are expected to communicate effectively across cultures and engage actively in collaborative programmes organized with partner universities through formal agreements.

HOW TO APPLY
------------
1. Choose what and when to study
2. Choose where to study
3. Fill up Application form (available on UMPSA website)
4. Head over to CACM (Centre for Academic and Competency Management) for the next step

REQUIREMENTS AND ELIGIBILITY
-----------------------------
- Currently enrolled full-time in an undergraduate programme at UMPSA
- Proficient in English for academic and social communication
- Maintain a minimum CGPA of 3.5 or equivalent
- Adhere to all policies and regulations of UMPSA or the host university
- Cover all personal expenses incurred during the mobility programme
- Represent UMPSA throughout the programme

PROGRAMME FEES
--------------
- Free waiver for local students who select universities under UMPSA MOU/MOA
- Students are responsible for all living expenses, incidentals, and travel costs
- Students who wish to enter an exchange programme outside UMPSA's MOU/MOA are required to pay fees set by the selected university

ACCOMMODATION
-------------
- Visit your chosen university's website regarding accommodation services
- Students can apply for accommodation services through the host university
- Contact the host university for further information

INTAKE CALENDAR
---------------
- Postgraduate (Research): Available on UMPSA website
- Postgraduate (Course Work): Available on UMPSA website
- Undergraduate: Available on UMPSA website
