UMPSA Registrar's Office (Jabatan Pendaftar)
==============================================

Website: https://pendaftar.umpsa.edu.my/

OVERVIEW
--------
The Registrar's Office (Jabatan Pendaftar) manages administrative services for UMPSA including talent management, talent development, governance and policy, security management, and facility-related issues.

DIVISIONS
---------

1. Centre for Talent Management
   - Staff recruitment and management
   - Human resource administration

2. Centre for Talent Development
   - Staff training and development
   - Professional development programmes

3. Centre for Governance and Policy
   - Ensuring clear guidelines for decision-making
   - Authority and actions within UMPSA
   - Policy development and implementation

4. Centre for Security Management
   - Campus security
   - Safety management

5. Facility Management
   - Seamless management of facility-related issues
   - Well-being of UMPSA residents
   - Key support system for growth of UMPSA staff

UMPSA STAFF STRENGTH (approximate)
-----------------------------------
- Academicians: Available on portal
- Administrators: Available on portal
- Total staff: Available on portal

SERVICES FOR STUDENTS
---------------------
- Student registration
- Academic records management
- Transcript issuance
- Certificate/degree verification
- Convocation management
- Student status letters
- Transfer and withdrawal processing
