UMPSA JHEPA - Student Affairs Department
==========================================

Website: https://jhepa.umpsa.edu.my/
Full name: Jabatan Hal Ehwal Pelajar dan Alumni (Department of Student Affairs and Alumni)

OVERVIEW
--------
JHEPA manages student welfare, development, and alumni relations at UMPSA. The department oversees various aspects of student life including welfare services, student development programmes, clubs and societies, sports, and alumni engagement.

ADAB APPLICATION (Student Mobile App)
--------------------------------------
The ADAB app provides students access to:
- Latest information on academics
- Student welfare
- Student finances
- Facilities available at UMPSA
- Online application features (zakat, e-complaints, etc.)
- Connection to department's official portals
- Government agencies and banks
- Private online payments
