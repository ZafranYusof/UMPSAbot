UMPSA Health Centre (Pusat Kesihatan Universiti - PKU)
=======================================================
Source: https://pku.umpsa.edu.my, https://mygreen.umpsa.edu.my
Last updated: 2026

OVERVIEW
========
University Health Centre (PKU) is a one-stop centre under the scope of primary health care for the UMPSA community. Both Gambang and Pekan campuses have one centre each.

Currently operating with 5 doctors and 34 other staff members.

HISTORY
=======
- Established in August 2004 under supervision of JHEPA
- Operations started with one Doctor & one Senior Nurse
- September 2007 to August 2009: no doctor in charge for clinic
- 2008: Operations moved to current infrastructure at Gambang Campus
- End of 2009: Three doctors joined the clinic
- 1st January 2010: Transformed into PTJ (independent department)
- 2010: Temporary Pekan clinic in FKM building
- 2017: New clinic structure became fully operational at Pekan campus
