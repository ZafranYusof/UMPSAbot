GENERAL INFORMATION
- Full Name: Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah (UMPSA)
- Previously known as: Kolej Universiti Kejuruteraan dan Teknologi Malaysia (KUKTEM), established 2002
- Location: 26600 Pekan, Pahang, Malaysia
- Tel: +609 431 5000
- Fax: +609 431 5555
- Email: pro@umpsa.edu.my
- Type: Public University (MTUN - Malaysian Technical University Network)
- Focus: Technical and Vocational Education Training (TVET), Engineering, Technology
- Employability Rate: 99.1% (as of April 2025)
- Campuses: Pekan, Gambang

ACADEMIC FOCUS
- Higher TVET (Technical and Vocational Education Training)
- STEAM Education (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics)
- Bachelor's degree in Technology (B.Tech) launched June 25, 2019
- 8 Technological Engineering Faculties
