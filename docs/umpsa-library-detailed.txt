UMPSA Library Services

Website: https://library.umpsa.edu.my

Services:

1. DISCOVERY@LIB - "Your one-stop platform to search, discover, and access everything in one place!"

2. eLib System (Digital Library):
   - eBook collection
   - Thesis collection
   - Open Shelf
   - CD & DVD
   - Reference materials
   - PTC (Professional Training Centre materials)
   - Special Collections
   - FYP (Final Year Project) reports

3. Inter Library Loan (ILL) & eDDS Services:
   ILL and eDDS services are offered to all registered users. The library assists users in retrieving materials either in its original form or copies from the library collection regardless if it is available or not.

4. UMPSA Institutional Repository (UMPSA-IR):
   - Article/item deposit for academicians
   - For postgraduates: submit articles or conference papers via email
   - Contact: amelia@umpsa.edu.my or norsaini@umpsa.edu.my

5. Digital Book Borrowing (eLIB eBook Portal):
   Users can borrow and read books digitally with 3 easy steps:
   1. Register account
   2. Borrow book
   3. Read book

6. Computer Labs for Students with Disabilities:
   Available at both Pekan and Gambang campuses with disability access.

7. Video Tutorials available for:
   - How to deposit items in UMPSA-IR
   - Inter-library loan applications
   - MYJURNAL searching & browsing
   - SCOPUS citation overview

8. Research Support:
   - Open Science resources
   - Search tools
   - Publication support
