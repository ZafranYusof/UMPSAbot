UMPSA Disability Services (OkUMP - Disability Services Unit)
=============================================================
Source: https://umpsa.edu.my/en/student-disability
Last updated: 2026

OVERVIEW
========
UMPSA has designated the Disability Services Unit (OkUMP) to determine eligibility for reasonable accommodations (academic adjustments, auxiliary aids, and modifications) for students with disabilities. This unit acts as a governance coordinator to create an inclusive and conducive campus environment/ecosystem for disabled people.

OkUMP coordinates efficient and effective management of disabled people at all levels of the university administration.

FACILITIES & SERVICES
=====================

Computer Labs for Students with Disabilities:
- Available at both UMP Pekan and Gambang campuses
- Reservation: https://umplibrary.ump.edu.my/index.php/en/self-help/facilities/computer-lab

Screen Reader Support:
- Converts text on screen into speech for visually impaired students
- NVDA screen reader available (press "Ctrl" to stop reading)
- Users can navigate by landmarks, titles, headings, links, buttons, or images
- Shortcut keys available for various applications

Disability-Friendly Campus:
- Most buildings have access for disabled people
- University constantly being built, reconstructed, adjusted, and adapted to needs of students with physical disabilities
- Students with disabilities live in dormitory rooms adapted to their needs
- Companions can be accommodated together
- Many academic programmes are flexible (distance learning, various forms of examination)
- University provides learning/educational tools and devices

Sign Language Interpreter / Note-takers:
- Available for hearing impaired students
- Assisted by Disability Service Unit
