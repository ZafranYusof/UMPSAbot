UMPSA Student Disability Services

Website: https://umpsa.edu.my/en/student-disability

UMP Disability Services Unit (OkUMP):
UMPSA has designated the Disability Services Unit (OkUMP) to determine eligibility for reasonable accommodations (academic adjustments, auxiliary aids, and modification) for students with disabilities. This unit acts as a governance coordinator to create an inclusive and conducive campus environment/ecosystem for disabled people, coordinating efficient and effective management of disabled people at all levels of the university administration.

Services Available:

1. Computer Labs for Students with Disabilities:
   Available at both UMPSA Pekan and Gambang campuses with disability access.
   Reservation: https://umplibrary.ump.edu.my/index.php/en/self-help/facilities/computer-lab

2. Screen Reader Technology:
   - Converts text on the screen into speech for visually impaired students
   - Available screen readers can be downloaded and installed
   - Can be set to automatically turn on with computer
   - Uses keyboard shortcuts for navigation (e.g., "Ctrl" to stop reading in NVDA)

3. Disability Friendly Campus:
   - Most buildings have access for disabled people
   - University is constantly being built, reconstructed, adjusted, and adapted to needs of students with physical disabilities
   - Students with disabilities live in dormitory rooms adapted to their needs
   - Companions can be accommodated together
   - Many academic programmes are flexible (distance learning, various forms of examination)
   - University provides learning/educational tools and devices

4. Sign Language Interpreter or Note-takers:
   Available for hearing impaired students, assisted by Disability Service Unit.
