Welcome to Admission Portal of Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah
- 16th Public University in Malaysia
- Formerly known as Universiti Malaysia Pahang

UMPSA offers a wide range of practical-based tertiary education programmes in engineering and technology. Its application-oriented curriculum integrates theory and practice in the concept of a teaching factory, emphasizing experiential and action learning, task oriented and problem solving.
