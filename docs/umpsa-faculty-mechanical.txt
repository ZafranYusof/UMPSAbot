Faculty of Mechanical and Automotive Engineering Technology (FTKMA) - UMPSA

Website: https://ftkma.umpsa.edu.my

We offer Engineering, Engineering Technology, and Technology programmes in Mechanical and Automotive fields.

We are an established Mechanical and Automotive Engineering faculty, which is recognised by the QS World University Ranking.

Academics:

Undergraduates:
Our mission is to produce knowledgeable students with good practical skills that enable them to adapt very quickly in the industry. Our curriculums are also tailored to ensure the students have the ability to develop products, work in a team and communicate effectively.

Postgraduates:
Engineers who are keen to upgrade their knowledge are welcomed to join our master by coursework programmes. In addition, we also offer research-based programmes (Master's and PhD) for those who prefer experimental and/or simulation work than taking classes.

Programmes:
- Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering Technology programmes
- Postgraduate programmes (Master's and PhD)
- Accredited by the Board of Engineers Malaysia (BEM)

Research:
- Journal of Mechanical Engineering and Sciences (JMES) - international, peer-reviewed platinum open-access journal
- Scope: analytical, computational modeling, structural mechanics, metal forming, advanced materials, impact mechanics, fluid mechanics, robotics, tribology, thermodynamics, and materials processing

Events:
- 8th International Conference on Mechanical Engineering Research (ICMER 2025)
- Postgraduate Research Colloquium
- Industrial Design Project (IDP) Open Day
