PROGRAMME LEVELS
----------------
UMPSA offers programmes at the following levels:
- Foundation (Asasi)
- Diploma
- Bachelor's Degree (Undergraduate)
- Postgraduate - Course Work (Master's)
- Postgraduate - Research (Master's and PhD)

IMPORTANT NOTES
---------------
- Each programme level has its own academic calendar
- Calendar includes semester dates, registration periods, examination weeks, and holidays
- Senate minutes contain decisions regarding academic calendar changes
- Students should refer to their specific programme level calendar
- Calendar is subject to changes approved by Senate
