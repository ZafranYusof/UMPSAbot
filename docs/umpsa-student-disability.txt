STUDENT WITH DISABILITY SERVICES - UMPSA
==========================================

Source: https://umpsa.edu.my/en/student-disability

UMP DISABILITY SERVICES UNIT (OkUMP):
- Determines eligibility for reasonable accommodations (academic adjustments, auxiliary aids, and modifications)
- Acts as governance coordinator to create an inclusive and conducive campus environment for disabled people
- Coordinates management of disabled people at all levels of university administration

FACILITIES:
1. Computer Labs for Students with Disabilities
   - Available at UMP Pekan and Gambang campuses
   - Reservation: https://umplibrary.ump.edu.my/index.php/en/self-help/facilities/computer-lab

2. Screen Reader Software
   - Converts text on screen into speech for visually impaired students
   - NVDA recommended (press "Ctrl" to stop reading)
   - Users can navigate by landmarks, titles, headings, links, buttons, or images

3. Disability-Friendly Campus
   - Most buildings have access for disabled people
   - Dormitory rooms adapted to needs, with companion accommodation possible
   - Distance learning and various examination forms available
   - Learning/educational tools and devices provided

4. Sign Language Interpreter / Note-takers
   - Available for hearing impaired students
   - Assisted by Disability Service Unit
