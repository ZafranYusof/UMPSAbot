PUSAT SEJAHTERA (Wellness Centre)
==================================
Director: Ts. Dr. Mohd Adnin bin Hamidi
Part of: JHEPA (Jabatan Hal Ehwal Pelajar & Alumni)

The Pusat Sejahtera (Wellness Centre) provides comprehensive wellness and counselling services to UMPSA students.

SERVICES
========
- Counselling services (individual and group)
- Mental health support
- Psychological assessments
- Crisis intervention
- Peer counselling programmes
- Wellness programmes and workshops
- Stress management
- Personal development counselling
- Academic counselling support
