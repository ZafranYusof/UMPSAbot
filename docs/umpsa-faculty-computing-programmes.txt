UMPSA Faculty of Computing - Programme Information
====================================================
Source: https://fk.umpsa.edu.my/index.php/academic/undergraduate
Last updated: 2026

OVERVIEW
========
Faculty of Computing (formerly known as Faculty of Computer Systems & Software Engineering) was established on 16 February 2002 to produce knowledgeable, high-skilled and competitive graduates within the sphere of software engineering, system and computer network.

Vision: "To be a world class competency-based faculty in computer technology"

Mission: "We provide computer technology education, high-quality research and consultation in line with the needs of industries through excellent academic programs by providing a conducive environment to produce creative and innovative human capital"

HISTORY
=======
- 16 February 2002: Faculty of Computer Systems & Software Engineering established
- 01 August 2019: Renamed to Faculty of Computing, became part of College of Computing and Applied Sciences
- 01 June 2020: Moved to own 5-storey building in UMPSA Pekan Campus

ACADEMIC FOCUS
==============
The program concentrates on teaching and building computer systems and software engineering knowledge towards Industrial Revolution (IR) 4.0.

Initial fields:
- Software Engineering
- Networking

RESEARCH GROUPS
===============
1. Network & Security
2. Modeling & Simulation
3. Data Mining & Knowledge Management
4. Graphic & Image Processing

These support university's focus groups (Manufacturing & Automotive and Chemical & Biotechnology).

RESEARCH AREAS
==============
- Information Systems
- Software Engineering
- Computer Systems
- Communication Systems
- Graphic and Multimedia Technology

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMMES
=========================
(Based on known UMPSA Computing programmes)
- Bachelor of Computer Science (Computer Systems & Networking) with Honours
- Bachelor of Computer Science (Software Engineering) with Honours
- Bachelor of Computer Science (Graphic & Multimedia Technology) with Honours
- Bachelor of Technology (Computer Systems) with Honours (B.Tech)

FACILITIES
==========

- High-quality laboratories
- Personable learning atmosphere
