OVERVIEW
--------
UMPSA has designated the Disability Services Unit (OkUMP) to determine eligibility for reasonable accommodations (academic adjustments, auxiliary aids, and modifications) for students with disabilities.

This unit acts as a governance coordinator to create an inclusive and conducive campus environment/ecosystem for disabled people, coordinating efficient and effective management of disabled people at all levels of the university administration.

SERVICES PROVIDED
-----------------

1. REASONABLE ACCOMMODATIONS
   - Academic adjustments
   - Auxiliary aids
   - Modifications to learning environment

2. COMPUTER LABS FOR STUDENTS WITH DISABILITIES
   - Available at UMP Pekan and Gambang campuses
   - Disability-accessible facilities
   - Reservation available online

3. SCREEN READER SUPPORT
   - Converts text on screen into speech for visually impaired students
   - Available screen readers can be downloaded and installed
   - Can be set to automatically turn on when computer powers on
   - Key shortcuts available (e.g., NVDA: press "Ctrl" to stop reading)
   - Users can navigate by landmarks, titles, headings, links, buttons, or images

4. SIGN LANGUAGE INTERPRETER / NOTE-TAKERS
   - Available for hearing impaired students
   - Assisted by Disability Service Unit

DISABILITY-FRIENDLY CAMPUS
---------------------------
- Most buildings have access for disabled people
- University is constantly being built, reconstructed, adjusted, and adapted to needs of students with physical disabilities
- Students with disabilities live in dormitory rooms adapted to their needs
- Possibility for companions to be accommodated together
- Many academic programmes are flexible
- Distance learning options available
- Various forms of examination accommodations
- University provides learning/educational tools and devices

PHILOSOPHY
----------
Disability is not an obstacle to becoming a member of the UMP community. Students with disabilities will always be heard and understood. They can always participate and contribute in the process of making comfortable conditions for learning, studying, and living.
