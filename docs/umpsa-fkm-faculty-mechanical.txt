PROGRAM DITAWARKAN
================================================================================

PROGRAM SARJANA MUDA (UNDERGRADUATE):

1. Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering Technology with Honours
   - Tempoh: 4 tahun (8 semester)
   - Akreditasi: MQA, Board of Engineers Malaysia (BEM)
   - Fokus: Mechanical design, manufacturing, thermodynamics

2. Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering Technology (Automotive) with Honours
   - Tempoh: 4 tahun (8 semester)
   - Akreditasi: MQA, BEM
   - Fokus: Automotive systems, vehicle dynamics, powertrain

3. Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering Technology (Maintenance) with Honours
   - Tempoh: 4 tahun (8 semester)
   - Akreditasi: MQA, BEM
   - Fokus: Industrial maintenance, reliability engineering, condition monitoring

4. Bachelor of Manufacturing Engineering Technology with Honours
   - Tempoh: 4 tahun (8 semester)
   - Akreditasi: MQA, BEM
   - Fokus: Manufacturing processes, CNC, quality control

5. Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
   - Tempoh: 3 tahun (6 semester)
   - Fokus: Asas mekanikal, lukisan kejuruteraan, bengkel

PROGRAM PASCA SISWAZAH (POSTGRADUATE):

1. Master of Engineering Technology (Mechanical) - by Coursework
   - Tempoh: 1.5 - 2 tahun

2. Master of Science (Mechanical Engineering) - by Research
   - Tempoh: 2 - 4 tahun

3. Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Mechanical Engineering
   - Tempoh: 3 - 6 tahun
