UMPSA Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
=======================================

Q: What is UMPSA?
A: Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah (UMPSA) is a public technical university in Malaysia, formerly known as UMP. It was renamed in 2023 in honour of Al-Sultan Abdullah Ri'ayatuddin Al-Mustafa Billah Shah.

Q: Where is UMPSA located?
A: UMPSA has two campuses - Gambang Campus in Kuantan, Pahang and Pekan Campus in Pekan, Pahang.

Q: What are the faculties at UMPSA?
A: UMPSA has several faculties including:
- Faculty of Electrical & Electronics Engineering Technology (FTKEE)
- Faculty of Mechanical & Automotive Engineering Technology (FTKMA)
- Faculty of Chemical & Process Engineering Technology (FTKKI)
- Faculty of Civil Engineering Technology (FTKA)
- Faculty of Computing (FK)
- Faculty of Industrial Management (FIM)
- Faculty of Industrial Sciences & Technology (FIST)
- Faculty of Manufacturing & Mechatronic Engineering Technology (FTKPM)
- Centre for Foundation Studies (CFS)

Q: How do I register for courses?
A: Course registration is done through the Student Portal (SMP) at https://smp.umpsa.edu.my. Registration typically opens 2 weeks before the semester begins.

Q: What is the academic calendar?
A: UMPSA follows a semester system with two main semesters and one short semester. Semester 1 usually starts in September, Semester 2 in February, and the short semester in July.

Q: How do I check my exam results?
A: Results can be checked through the Student Portal (SMP) after the official release date announced by the Academic Office.

Q: What student facilities are available?
A: UMPSA provides various facilities including:
- Library (Perpustakaan)
- Sports Complex
- Student Activity Centre (SAC)
- Health Centre
- Cafeterias and food courts
- Mosque (Masjid)
- Residential colleges (Kolej Kediaman)

Q: How do I apply for hostel/residential college?
A: Hostel applications are made through the e-Residence system. Priority is given to first-year students and those from distant locations.

Q: What financial aid is available?
A: Students can apply for:
- PTPTN (National Higher Education Fund)
- JPA Scholarships
- MARA Scholarships
- University scholarships and bursaries
- Zakat funds
