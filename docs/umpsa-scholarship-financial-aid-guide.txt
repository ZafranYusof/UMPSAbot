UMPSA Scholarship and Financial Aid Guide
==========================================

OVERVIEW
--------
UMPSA provides various scholarships and financial aid options to support students throughout their studies. The university is committed to ensuring that financial constraints do not prevent deserving students from pursuing higher education.

TYPES OF FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
------------------------------

1. PTPTN (Perbadanan Tabung Pendidikan Tinggi Nasional)
   - National Higher Education Fund Corporation
   - Available to all Malaysian students
   - Loan-based financial assistance
   - Repayment begins after graduation
   - Apply online at: https://www.ptptn.gov.my
   - First-class honours graduates may receive loan conversion to scholarship

2. ZAKAT ASSISTANCE
   - Available through JHEPA
   - For eligible students (asnaf category)
   - Apply through ADAB app or JHEPA office

3. UMPSA SCHOLARSHIPS
   - Merit-based scholarships for outstanding students
   - Various categories based on academic achievement
   - Apply through university portal

4. EXTERNAL SCHOLARSHIPS
   - JPA (Public Service Department) scholarships
   - MARA scholarships
   - State government scholarships (Yayasan Pahang, etc.)
   - Corporate/industry scholarships
   - Foundation scholarships

5. EMERGENCY FINANCIAL AID
   - For students facing unexpected financial difficulties
   - Apply through JHEPA
   - Short-term assistance available

6. CAMPUS PANTRY (FOOD BANK)
   - Free meals for students in need
   - Website: https://campuspantry.umpsa.edu.my/
   - 2,500 individuals served weekly
   - 8,000 meals distributed weekly

HOW TO APPLY FOR FINANCIAL AID
-------------------------------
1. Check eligibility criteria for each type of aid
2. Prepare required documents (income statements, academic records, etc.)
3. Submit application through the respective portal or office
4. Follow up on application status

IMPORTANT NOTES
---------------
- Apply for financial aid early (before semester starts)
- Keep all receipts and documentation
- Report any changes in financial circumstances
- Maintain good academic standing to retain scholarships
- Check deadlines for each type of financial aid
