WELCOME MESSAGE
===============
UMPSA is a vibrant, forward-thinking institution committed to academic excellence, impactful research, and meaningful industry collaboration.

UMPSA aspires to be a leading provider of engineering and technology-based education, research, and innovation.

LONG-TERM ASPIRATIONS
======================
1. To become a recognised Eco-University by 2030, championing sustainability and environmental stewardship
2. To position UMPSA as the ASEAN Advanced TVET Hub by 2030, driving excellence in technical and vocational education and training across the region
3. To establish as a distinguished technical university by 2050, known globally for world-class programmes and innovation-driven impact

SPIROS PHILOSOPHY
=================
A holistic and balanced approach that empowers every member of the UMPSA community:
- Skills
- Perfection

- Resilience
- Optimisation
- Sustainability

Guiding slogan: "Technology for Society"
