UMPSA Research Grants and Funding

Website: https://research.umpsa.edu.my

Internal Research Grants Available:

1. UMP Fundamental Research Grant
   - For young researchers to support fundamental/basic research
   - Produces new knowledge, theories, concepts and ideas
   - Provides exposure and nurtures researchers' ability in research project management
   - Encourages research cultivation and preparation for external grants

2. UMP Distinguished Research Grant
   - For experienced researchers to drive and strengthen research quality
   - Contributes to human capital development
   - Increases university publication output
   - Implements collaborative outreach with international parties, industries, and agencies

3. UMP International Publication Grant
   - Strengthens researcher's ability in impactful, high quality indexed publications
   - Requires international collaboration
   - Strengthens collaboration between educational institutions

4. UMP Product Development Grant
   - Produces a prototype or product that can function
   - Combines expertise of internal researchers and existing market components
   - Must meet community/market needs and solve universal problems

5. UMP Made In UMP Grant
   - Uses UMP's technology, expertise, and resources
   - Develops new products/innovations or improvements to existing products
   - Supports TVET activities at UMP
   - May involve one or more centres of responsibility or research domains

6. UMP First In The World Grant
   - Supports research for new discovery or high-impact technology
   - Something never explored or produced by any party in the world
   - Must have high potential for commercialization
   - Brings great benefits to the community

7. UMP Social Science Research Grant
   - Supports research in social sciences, humanities, management, and economics
   - Toward more relevant social impact to industry and global community

8. UMP Lab2Market Grant
   - Helps researchers develop products/technologies from research outputs
   - High potential for commercialization activities
   - Involves market validation, product optimization, technology effectiveness demonstration
   - Minimum Technology Readiness Level (TRL) of 6 required
   - Activities include: product optimization, manufacturing, system development, market testing, packaging, product manual development
