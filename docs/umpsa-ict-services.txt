UMPSA ICT Services (DiTec - Digital Technology Centre)

OVERVIEW
========
DiTec (Digital Technology Centre) is UMPSA's ICT services department, providing digital infrastructure and support for students and staff.

SERVICES FOR STUDENTS
=====================

WiFi / Internet Access:
- EDUROAM - University WiFi network available across both campuses
- Guide available: "Connect to EDUROAM" (downloadable from DiTec website)

Software (Free for Students & Staff):
- Adobe Creative Cloud (CC) 2020 - Collection of 20+ creative desktop and mobile apps including Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat Pro
  - Installation guide available on DiTec website
- Microsoft 365 (formerly Office 365) - Create, share, and collaborate for free with Office on the web
  - Access via: https://umplive.umpsa.edu.my/

Account Management:
- Self Password Reset guide available
- Java Configuration for IMS (Integrated Management System)

Other Resources:
- Printer IP address change manual
- Digital support downloads

STUDENT PORTALS & SYSTEMS
==========================
- E-community: Student portal for hostel applications, student services
- IMS (Integrated Management System): Academic management
- ADAB App: Mobile app for student information (available on iOS and Android)
  - iOS: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ump-adab/id1574433441
  - Android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=my.edu.ump.adab
  - Features: academics info, student welfare, student finances, facilities, zakat applications, e-complaints, connections to official portals, government agencies, banks, and private online payments
