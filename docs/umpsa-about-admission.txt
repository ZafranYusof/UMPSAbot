UMPSA - ABOUT THE UNIVERSITY

HISTORY
- Established as a technical university in 2002
- Originally named Kolej Universiti Kejuruteraan & Teknologi Malaysia (KUKTEM), established 16 February 2002
- Renamed to Universiti Malaysia Pahang (UMP) effective 1 February 2007
- Rebranded to Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah (UMPSA) effective 14 April 2023
- Based on Universiti Malaysia Pahang Order (Incorporation) (Amendment) 2023 (P.U. (A) 116/2023)
- 16th Public University in Malaysia
- Part of Malaysia Technical University Network (MTUN)

CHANCELLOR
- His Royal Highness, The Sultan of Pahang, Al-Sultan Abdullah Ri'ayatuddin Al-Mustafa Billah Shah

VISION
- Leading technological university
- Excellence through academic empowerment and impactful research
- Produce outstanding graduates as quality human capital

FOCUS AREAS
- Engineering and technology-based technical programmes
- High-level Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET)
- Application-oriented curriculum (theory + practice)
- Teaching factory concept
- Experiential and action learning

INTERNATIONAL COLLABORATIONS (Dual Degree)
- Karlsruhe University of Applied Sciences (HKA), Germany
- Mannheim University of Applied Sciences (HMA), Germany
- BJTU, China
- Tianjin University, China
- Kazakh National Agrarian Research University (KazNARU), Kazakhstan
- Hanyang University Erica Campus, South Korea
- UMPSA is the only public university (UA) with 10 dual degree programmes

RESEARCH
- Ranked among the best in research and innovation within MTUN and Non-Research University (Non-RU) classifications
- Collaborates with local industries for industry-related applications
- Research enriches teaching and learning modules
- Promotes commercialisation of research output and products
