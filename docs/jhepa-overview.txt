JABATAN HAL EHWAL PELAJAR DAN ALUMNI (JHEPA) UMPSA
===================================================

Source: https://jhepa.umpsa.edu.my/index.php/en/

JHEPA (Jabatan Hal Ehwal Pelajar dan Alumni) is the department responsible for student affairs and alumni at UMPSA (Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah).

KEY SERVICES:
- Student welfare management
- Financial aid coordination (zakat, bantuan sara diri)
- Student development programs
- Alumni relations

ADAB APPLICATION (Mobile App):
- Provides students access to latest information on academics, student welfare, student finances, and facilities
- Online application features: zakat applications, e-complaints, and others
- Connects students with official portals at UMPSA, government agencies, banks, and private online payments
- Available on:
  - Apple App Store: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ump-adab/id1574433441
  - Google Play Store: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=my.edu.ump.adab

STUDENT WELFARE ASSISTANCE (from Linktree @umphepacare):
- eKupon Kaseh Rahmah (Kupon Makan) - free food coupons for B40 students
- Skim Perantisan Siswa (Job on Campus) - campus employment scheme
- Bantuan Komputer Riba - laptop assistance for students in need
