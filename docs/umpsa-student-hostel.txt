UMPSA Student Accommodation (Residen Pelajar / Hostel)
=======================================================
Source: https://jhepa.umpsa.edu.my/index.php/ms/about/department/perkhidmatan-pelajar/residen-pelajar
Last updated: 2026

OVERVIEW
========
UMPSA provides student residential accommodation managed by JHEPA (Jabatan Hal Ehwal Pelajar & Alumni). Students apply for hostel accommodation through E-community > Student Resident > Hostel Application.

RESIDENTIAL COLLEGES (RESIDEN PELAJAR)
======================================

Residen Pelajar 1 (RP1)
------------------------
- For female students
- Priority given to students in Badan Unit Bruniform (uniformed bodies)
- Location: Gambang Campus

Residen Pelajar 2 (RP2)
------------------------
- For female students
- Priority given to students in Badan Unit Bruniform
- Also has: Ruang Rumah Berkeluarga Pascasiswazah Antarabangsa (International Postgraduate Family Housing)
- Location: Gambang Campus

Residen Pelajar 3 (RP3)
------------------------
- For female students
- Priority given to students in Badan Unit Bruniform
- Location: Gambang Campus

Residen Pelajar 4 (RP4)
------------------------
- For female students
- Priority given to students in Badan Unit Bruniform
- Location: Gambang Campus

Residen Pelajar 5 (RP5)
------------------------
- For students
- Priority given to students in Badan Unit Bruniform
- Location: Pekan Campus

BASIC ROOM EQUIPMENT (All Residences)
=====================================
- Katil & tilam (Bed & mattress)
- Meja Belajar (Study desk)
- Almari Pakaian (Wardrobe)
- Penyidai Tuala (Towel rack)

SHARED FACILITIES (All Residences)
===================================
- Tandas setiap tingkat (Toilets on every floor)
- Dobi (Laundry)
- Student Lounge
- Gym
- Taman Rekreasi (Recreation park)

HOW TO APPLY
============
1. Log in to E-community portal
2. Navigate to: Student Resident > Hostel Application
3. For special pathway categories and appeal applications, apply online
4. Appeal applications are subject to availability displayed on each student's e-community

Note: Room selection for special pathway categories and appeal applications must be done online through E-community.
