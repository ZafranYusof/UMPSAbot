UMPSA Campus Pantry (Food Bank)
================================

Website: https://campuspantry.umpsa.edu.my/

OVERVIEW
--------
The UMP Food Bank actively fights hunger and feeds hope for UMPSA students. Campus Pantry is a food bank initiative that provides meals and food support to students in need.

KEY STATISTICS
--------------
- 2,500 individuals served each week
- 8,000 meals distributed weekly

HOW IT WORKS
------------
- RM5 minimum donation provides 1 meal per student
- Volunteers welcome (groups and individuals)
- Donations accepted - check pantry stocks on website

VOLUNTEERING
------------
UMP Students under Majlis Perwakilan Pelajar (MPP) and Kelab Penyayang UMP are required to work during Campus Pantry operating hours, including:
- Donation sorting shifts
- Food sorting shifts
- Food distributions

HOW TO HELP
-----------
1. DONATE: RM5 minimum provides 1 meal
2. VOLUNTEER: Many opportunities available for groups and individuals
3. CONTRIBUTE FOOD: Bring what you can to the Pantry
