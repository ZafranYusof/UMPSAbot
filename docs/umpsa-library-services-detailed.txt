SERVICES OVERVIEW
-----------------

1. DISCOVERY@LIB
   - One-stop platform to search, discover, and access everything in one place
   - Access via: https://efind.ump.edu.my

2. eLIB eBOOK PORTAL
   - Borrow and read books digitally
   - Steps: (1) Register account (2) Borrow book (3) Read book
   - Available to all UMPSA staff and students

3. INTER LIBRARY LOAN (ILL)
   - Service to source materials (printed or electronic) not available in UMPSA Library
   - Practiced between libraries in Malaysia
   - Materials: books or journal articles
   - Books must be returned within 1 month from date of borrowing
   - Check availability first at https://efind.ump.edu.my
   - Apply online: https://forms.gle/smbfEsFAPcBKcQT96

4. ELECTRONIC DOCUMENT DELIVERY (eDDS)
   - Request copies of book chapters or pages from UMPSA library collection
   - For users who are not near campus
   - Material will be scanned and sent to user
   - Limited to required chapters/pages only
   - Strictly for UMPSA staff and students
   - Apply online: https://bit.ly/eDDSprint

5. UMPSA INSTITUTIONAL REPOSITORY (UMPSA-IR)
   - Deposit articles/items to UMPSA-IR
   - For postgraduates: email submissions to amelia@umpsa.edu.my or norsaini@umpsa.edu.my
   - Officers will deposit and confirm via email
