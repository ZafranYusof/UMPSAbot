SUSTAINABILITY CLUB
===================

Econexus Club
- Student-led sustainability organisation at UMPSA
- Engages students in environmental conservation, climate awareness, and sustainable development
- Activities include: tree and mangrove planting, waste management awareness campaigns, community-based environmental initiatives
- Collaborates with university departments and external organisations
- Provides leadership skills development and practical experience

STUDENT REPRESENTATIVE COUNCIL (MPP)
=====================================

Majlis Perwakilan Pelajar (MPP) UMPSA consists of 28 elected student representatives chosen through official student elections.

Session 2025/2026:
- Led by Adam Al-Wafiy bin Muhammad Zailani (Faculty of Chemical & Process Engineering Technology)
- 3 main departments:
  1. Jabatan Komunikasi & Korporat (Communications & Corporate)
  2. Jabatan Pembangunan Mahasiswa (Student Development)
  3. Jabatan Perkhidmatan Mahasiswa (Student Services)
