UMPSA Student Exchange Programme - Inbound
============================================

Website: https://www.umpsa.edu.my/en/international/inbound
Contact: mobility@umpsa.edu.my

OVERVIEW
--------
Experience UMPSA beyond the classroom. International mobility and exchange programmes offer students the opportunity to study in a dynamic academic environment while discovering Malaysia's rich culture and global perspectives.

Through this programme, students enhance academic knowledge, intercultural competence, and personal growth. Courses completed during the exchange may be transferred and recognised upon successful completion, subject to academic approval.

HOW TO APPLY
------------
1. Select your preferred courses and study period
2. Complete the online Application Form (available on UMPSA website)
3. After submission, the International Office will review your application and contact you regarding approval and confirmation

REQUIREMENTS AND ELIGIBILITY
-----------------------------
- Currently enrolled as a full-time student at your home university
- Possess strong English skills for effective communication
- Agree to comply with the rules and regulations of UMPSA
- Take responsibility for all personal expenses during the mobility program

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
------------------
- Copy of applicant's passport information page (valid for at least 18 months from intake date)
- Academic transcripts
- Passport-size photograph with white background
- Confirmation letter from home institution
- Completed Inbound Application form

Submit all documents via email to: mobility@umpsa.edu.my

SUMMER SCHOOL (EDUTOURISM) - INBOUND NON-CREDIT
-------------------------------------------------
Requirements & Eligibility:
- Enrolled full-time at your home university
- Commit to following rules and regulations of UMPSA or host university
- Accept responsibility for all personal expenses related to the programme
