Website: https://jhepa.umpsa.edu.my

ADAB Application:
This app provides students access to the latest information on academics, student welfare, student finances, and facilities available at UMPSA. It also provides online application features such as zakat, e-complaints and others. It also connects students with the department's official portals at UMPSA, government agencies and banks, and private online payments.

Download ADAB App:
- iOS: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ump-adab/id1574433441
- Android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=my.edu.ump.adab

Features:
- Latest information on academics
- Student welfare services
- Student finances information
- Facilities information
- Zakat application
- E-complaints
- Connection to official portals, government agencies, banks, and private online payments

Student Life:
- Student lifestyle activities
- Sports achievements
- Annual reports available on website
- JHEPA YouTube channel for updates
