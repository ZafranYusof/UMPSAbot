UMPSA Academic Guide
====================

Credit Hours and Course Load
-----------------------------
- Minimum credit hours per semester: 12
- Maximum credit hours per semester: 21
- Dean's List students may register up to 23 credit hours
- Short semester: Maximum 10 credit hours

Grading System
--------------
Grade | Point | Marks
A+    | 4.00  | 90-100
A     | 4.00  | 80-89
A-    | 3.67  | 75-79
B+    | 3.33  | 70-74
B     | 3.00  | 65-69
B-    | 2.67  | 60-64
C+    | 2.33  | 55-59
C     | 2.00  | 50-54
C-    | 1.67  | 45-49
D+    | 1.33  | 40-44
D     | 1.00  | 35-39
E     | 0.00  | 0-34

Academic Standing
-----------------
- Good Standing: CGPA >= 2.00
- Conditional: CGPA 1.75 - 1.99
- Academic Probation: CGPA < 1.75
- Dean's List: GPA >= 3.50 (minimum 12 credit hours, no failed courses)
- Vice-Chancellor's List: CGPA >= 3.50 for all semesters

Important Policies
------------------
1. Attendance: Minimum 80% attendance required for all courses
2. Plagiarism: Zero tolerance policy. First offense may result in course failure.
3. Add/Drop: Course add/drop allowed within first 2 weeks of semester
4. Withdrawal: Course withdrawal allowed until week 7 (W grade, no GPA impact)
5. Repeat Course: Students may repeat failed courses. Best grade is taken.

Final Year Project (FYP)
------------------------
- FYP 1: Proposal and literature review (Semester 7)
- FYP 2: Implementation and thesis (Semester 8)
- Supervisor assigned based on research interest
- Minimum 2 meetings per month with supervisor
- Thesis submission deadline: Week 14 of semester

Industrial Training
-------------------
- Duration: Minimum 16 weeks (one full semester)
- Typically in Semester 6 or 7
- Students must find placement and get faculty approval
- Log book must be maintained throughout training
- Final report and presentation required
