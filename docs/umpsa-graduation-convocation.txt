UMPSA Graduation and Convocation Information
==============================================

CONVOCATION CEREMONY
--------------------
UMPSA holds its convocation ceremony annually to celebrate the achievements of graduating students. The ceremony is a formal event where degrees, diplomas, and certificates are conferred.

Website: https://www.umpsa.edu.my/en/convocation

GRADUATION REQUIREMENTS
------------------------

For Bachelor's Degree (Undergraduate):
1. Complete all required credit hours for the programme
2. Achieve minimum CGPA of 2.00
3. Complete all compulsory courses (university, faculty, and programme courses)
4. Complete industrial training/internship (if required by programme)
5. Complete Final Year Project (FYP) / Thesis (if required)
6. Fulfill co-curricular requirements
7. Pass Bahasa Melayu requirement (for international students)
8. No outstanding disciplinary issues
9. Clear all financial obligations to the university

For Diploma:
1. Complete all required credit hours
2. Achieve minimum CGPA of 2.00
3. Complete all compulsory courses
4. Complete industrial training (if required)
5. Fulfill co-curricular requirements
6. Clear all financial obligations

For Postgraduate (Master's/PhD):
1. Complete all required coursework/research
2. Submit and defend thesis/dissertation
3. Pass viva voce examination
4. Make required corrections (if any)
5. Submit final corrected thesis
6. Clear all financial obligations

CONVOCATION PROCESS
-------------------
1. Eligibility check by Faculty/Department
2. Senate approval of graduating students list
3. Convocation registration (online)
4. Robe fitting and collection
5. Rehearsal (mandatory)
6. Convocation ceremony
7. Certificate/scroll collection

ACADEMIC REGALIA
----------------
- Graduation robes are provided/rented through the university
- Different colours for different faculties
- Robe fitting sessions scheduled before convocation

IMPORTANT NOTES
---------------
- Students must settle all fees and fines before graduation
- Return all library books and clear library dues
- Return hostel keys and clear hostel dues
- Complete exit survey/tracer study
- Update contact information for alumni records

DEAN'S LIST AND AWARDS
-----------------------
- Dean's List: Semester GPA of 3.50 and above (with minimum credit hours)
- Vice-Chancellor's Award: Highest CGPA in programme
- Royal Education Award: Outstanding academic achievement
- Various excellence awards presented during convocation

TRANSCRIPT AND CERTIFICATE
---------------------------
- Official transcript available from Registrar's Office
- Degree certificate/scroll presented during convocation
- Additional certified copies can be requested from Registrar's Office
- Verification letters available for employment purposes

ALUMNI
------
- Graduates automatically become UMPSA alumni
- Alumni network managed by JHEPA
- Alumni events and networking opportunities
- Success stories featured on JHEPA website
